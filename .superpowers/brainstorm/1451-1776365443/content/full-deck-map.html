Fleetora Pitch Deck — Full Screen Map

8 screens, Apple Keynote flow. Each screen = one full viewport. Dark radiant theme throughout.

1
The Opening — Scroll-Morph Hero
Cars scatter → line → circle formation. Center text: "The operations brain for modern car rental." Scroll morphs circle into bottom arc. Dark background, radiant blue glow on the circle. Sets the tone: this is not a PowerPoint.
ANIMATION: Existing scroll-morph-hero, reskinned dark
2
The Problem — "Your Fleet Is Flying Blind"
Full-screen dark. One sentence fades in, large and stark. Then three pain points appear one by one with staggered timing: idle cars, slow checkouts, WhatsApp chaos. Each with a subtle red pulse icon. Emotional gut-punch.
ANIMATION: Fade-in cascade, staggered 300ms
3
The Cost — Numbers That Hurt
Three massive count-up numbers on a dark canvas: "20+ min per checkout", "15% fleet sitting idle", "Revenue recognized weeks late". Numbers animate from 0 to final value. Below each: one line of context. Let the numbers do the talking.
ANIMATION: Count-up from 0, spring easing, 1.5s duration
4
The Reveal — "Meet Fleetora"
The emotional pivot. Dark screen, then the Fleetora wordmark scales up from the center with a radiant blue-violet glow bloom behind it. Subtitle fades in: "One command surface. Every branch. Every vehicle. Every decision." This is the moment they lean forward.
ANIMATION: Scale 0.8→1 with blur-to-sharp, glow pulse
5
Three Pillars — Intelligence / Speed / Depth
Three cards that appear one at a time as presenter scrolls. Each fills the screen with an icon, a bold title, and 2 lines of copy. "Intelligence is the interface" → "Operations at the speed of thought" → "Depth where it matters". Scroll advances through all three on the same screen.
ANIMATION: Horizontal slide or crossfade between pillars
6
Product Showcase — The Cockpit
Stylized product mockups of the HQ Cockpit, POS Dashboard, and Booking Detail. Each floats in on scroll with a subtle perspective tilt (like Apple showing a MacBook screen). Browser frame chrome around screenshots. Glow underneath. Small label for each: what the role sees.
ANIMATION: Perspective tilt entry, 3D transform, subtle shadow
7
Who It's For — Three Roles
Three columns: Branch Staff, Franchise Head, Finance Admin. Each with a role icon, what they care about, and their home screen in Fleetora. Appears as three glass-morphism cards. The client sees themselves in one of these roles.
ANIMATION: Stagger from left to right, glass card hover effects
8
The Close — "The Unfair Advantage"
Final screen. Dark, quiet, powerful. "Every booking becomes training data. Every return feeds back into pricing. Every decision sharpens the next one." Then the Fleetora wordmark, a single CTA: "Let's talk." with contact info. Leave them wanting more.
ANIMATION: Slow fade-in, minimal motion. Let the words breathe.
Design Constants (All Screens)
• Background: #0f172a (slate-900) with subtle radial gradient accents
• Typography: Geist Sans. Headlines 48-72px, semibold. Body 16-18px, regular.
• Accent: Blue #3b82f6 → Violet #8b5cf6 gradient for highlights and glows
• Transitions: Scroll-triggered, spring physics via Framer Motion
• Progress: Thin gradient progress bar at top of viewport
• Keyboard: Arrow keys / Space also advance screens (presenter-friendly)
